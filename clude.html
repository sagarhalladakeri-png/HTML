Skills Required for College Admission

For Science Stream

  • Strong foundation in Mathematics
  • Problem-solving abilities
  • Logical reasoning
  • Laboratory skills
  • Computer proficiency
  • Min. 60% in 10+2 (Science)

For Commerce Stream

  • Basic accounting knowledge
  • Business acumen
  • Communication skills
  • Numerical aptitude
  • Basic economics understanding
  • Min. 55% in 10+2 (Commerce)

For Arts/Humanities

  • Strong language skills
  • Critical thinking
  • Writing abilities
  • Cultural awareness
  • Research skills
  • Min. 50% in 10+2 (Any stream)

For Engineering (B.Tech)

  • PCM in 10+2 with min. 75%
  • JEE Main qualification
  • Strong analytical skills
  • Programming basics
  • Technical drawing
  • Innovation mindset

  • Domicile certificate (for state quota)
  • English proficiency
  • Basic computer knowledge
  • Valid entrance test score (if applicable)
